About Giriraj Steel Furniture

Giriraj Industries/Giri Steel Furniture is a Strategic Diversified Company, having efficient and result oriented work force in the field of manufacturing of Steel Furniture, Sheet metal work and Fabrication work with High tech Machineries.

Established in the year 2007, we Giriraj Industries/Giri Steel Furniture, are one of the trusted business entities involved in manufacturing and supplying an exquisite collection of manufacturing of Steel Furniture, Sheet metal work and Fabrication work. Tested on diverse stringent parameters, our products are corrosion resistant and are known for their contemporary design and perfect finishing. Owing to such attributes, our products are widely used in Industrial, offices and residences for furnishing and decoration purposes.

Our organization is successfully headed by the second generation mentor Mr. Ravindra S Giri with experience of this work since 1975, under whose guidance we have attained immense success. His sharp business acumen and wise management skills have enabled us to mark a remarkable position in the industry.
